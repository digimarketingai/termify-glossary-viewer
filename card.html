💬 討論問題Discussion Question

「甲賀」對中文玩家有意義嗎？英文玩家會否失去這層文化意涵？

Does "甲賀 (Koga)" have meaning for Chinese players? Do English players lose this cultural layer?

✅ 建議答案Suggested Answer

「甲賀」是日本著名忍者流派，中文保留此名稱能：

  • 強化忍者形象
  • 保留文化深度
  • 吸引熟悉日本文化的玩家

英文「Greninja」雖失去「甲賀」元素，但透過「Ninja」仍傳達忍者概念，且創譯名較易發音記憶。

"甲賀 (Koga)" is a famous Japanese ninja clan. Keeping it in Chinese:

  • Strengthens the ninja image
  • Preserves cultural depth
  • Appeals to players familiar with Japanese culture

English "Greninja" loses the "Koga" element, but still conveys the ninja concept through "Ninja", and the coined name is easier to pronounce and remember.

💬 討論問題Discussion Question

為何不譯為「藏牌」（2字）？字數會影響卡牌排版嗎？

Why not translate as "藏牌" (2 characters)? Does word count affect card layout?

✅ 建議答案Suggested Answer

「隱藏牌」優於「藏牌」的原因：

  • 「隱藏」語意更完整，強調「隱密」特性
  • 「藏牌」可能被誤解為「收藏的牌」
  • 三字結構更正式，符合遊戲術語風格

字數確實影響排版，但可讀性和準確性優先於節省空間。

"隱藏牌" is better than "藏牌" because:

  • "隱藏" has more complete meaning, emphasizing "concealment"
  • "藏牌" could be misunderstood as "collected cards"
  • Three-character structure is more formal, matching game terminology style

Word count does affect layout, but readability and accuracy take priority over saving space.

💬 討論問題Discussion Question

為何中英都保留「手裏劍/Shuriken」？譯為「飛鏢/Throwing Star」會有何不同？

Why do both Chinese and English keep "手裏劍/Shuriken"? What would be different if translated as "Throwing Star"?

✅ 建議答案Suggested Answer

保留「手裏劍/Shuriken」的原因：

  • 文化獨特性：手裏劍是日本忍者專屬武器，「飛鏢」太普通
  • 角色一致性：強化甲賀忍蛙的忍者身份
  • 玩家期待：寶可夢玩家熟悉日本文化元素
  • 品牌統一：全球版本保持一致風格

若譯為「飛鏢/Throwing Star」，會失去忍者特色，變得平淡普通。

Reasons to keep "手裏劍/Shuriken":

  • Cultural uniqueness: Shuriken is a ninja-specific weapon; "Throwing Star" is too generic
  • Character consistency: Reinforces Greninja's ninja identity
  • Player expectations: Pokémon players are familiar with Japanese cultural elements
  • Brand unity: Maintains consistent style across global versions

If translated as "Throwing Star", it would lose the ninja character and become bland.
